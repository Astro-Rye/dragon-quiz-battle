## Game Overview
Saiyan Quiz Battle is a Dragon Ball themed trivia game that challenges the players knowledge of Saiyan lore!
Each correct answer increases your "power level" and displays a feedback image showing whether you were right or wrong.
Ypur total score is saved to a high score file and the end of each game and is visible for players to see

## How to Play
1. **Launch the game**
- Run "SaiyanQuizGame.java" from the IDE
2. **Enter your player name**: in the text field at the bottom left
3. **Reach each question**: and select the answer you believe is correct.
4. Click **Submit Answer**.
- If correct your power increases, and a "Correct!" image appears as Goku Lore!
- If incorrect: your power decreases, and a "Wrong!" image appears as a Dragon Ball Z Meme 
5. Click **Next Question**: to move to the next round. 
6. After all questions:
- Your total power level is displayed
- Youll be asked if you want to **play again**.
7. **High scores** are stored in 'highscores.txt' so you can view them anytime by clicking **View High Scores.
